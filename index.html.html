Project Idea

Overview

The project plan is to develop a Raspberry Pi technology that can be installed in the sports car so that the one driving the car have a comfortable journey by easy diagnosis that gets started with just order of mouth. This will provide the experience of undisturbed driving and comfort with use of new technology. The project needs to be completed with proper market research and understanding the underlying cost structure for development and implementation of software. The feasibility of the project is considered to be 5 years tenure and the aim is to provide an experience to the owners that allow long term trust with sports car. The technology will allow getting work done easily without much movement and pressure to the body and watching favourite shows. The high class people find this luxury in the car and are ready to pay the huge cost associated with such cars

Motivation

The growth of demand for automobiles has grown with the inception of sports car that provides luxurious experience to the customers. The high status customers do not find it difficult or hard to pay the huge bill of the car as they get luxury while travelling. There are around 1.5 billion car owners in Australia and 90% of the population are interested in buying luxurious cars that provide them with high performance and luxury. The people are car enthusiastic and excited in buying luxurious vehicles that suits their status. The use of Raspberry Pi will increase the comfort of the customers that is associated with these luxury cars and is available with high feasibility in terms of cost and installation. This is one of the most effective solutions to this problem. It displays all car diagnostics easily (About Sports car overview, 2019).

Description

The Raspberry Pi technology to be used in the sports car will provide convenience to drivers through detection of traffic lights, pedestrian detection, vehicle and road sign detection so that cars can be made highly autonomous. It is inbuilt with a machine learning technology ad sensor that detects these and provides intimation to the driver through sounds so that control can be named better during situations of high speed. These are highly active and reliable technology that provides first hand information for better experience while driving. There is a camera that is located at the port of the car that foresees things for information to the one driving. This technology can also be used in the highly automated cars without drivers. It acts like a central processing unit in the car that provides right direction like a video streaming. The camera allows detecting the various parameters while driving the car (Raspberry Pi technology in car, 2019). The technology is designed to even notice and alert about the small parameters that comes on the way while car driving on the road. The highly reliable technology is safe and allows safe driving with less prone to accidents. The cars can be made more autonomous and safety level can be increased while driving.

Tools and Technologies

The hardware required in the installation of this latest technology in cars includes a USB Micro power supply which looks like a standard charger of mobile phone. An 8 GB class SD card of total quantity of 4pc is also needed for the installation of system. It also requires an Ethernet to ensure connectivity in the car and a memory card reader is installed in the system for processing of information. A good quality of power supply is ensured in the cars through proper use of cables to manage comfort and reliability of functions of the device. The software required for the installation of Raspberry Pi includes IP scanner, IOT platform and EdgeX package. The deployment of these hardware and software provides proper functioning of the Raspberry Pi in the cars to manage high level of comfort and luxury among the purchasers
